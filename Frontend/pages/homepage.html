Incident Awareness and Reporting Portal





Rapid Rescue Network provides a centralized forum for community members to exchange information, report crimes, and remain up to date on safety-related issues. To ensure inclusion, it usually contains elements like emergency contact information, data analysis capabilities, community involvement forums, criminal reporting, anonymous reporting alternatives, disaster management suggestions, and accessibility features. Through the utilization of technology, this portal enables law enforcement and civilians to communicate and collaborate, so enabling communities to proactively and effectively address safety issues.
